## PHASE 3: CHANNEL ERA (Second Rebirth)

### Channel Capacity Meta-Upgrades

#### 1. Bandwidth Expansion
- **Cost**: 2 capacity tokens per level
- **Effect**: +20% max throughput on all channels
- **Max Level**: 10

#### 2. Error Correction
- **Cost**: 3 capacity tokens
- **Effect**: Unlock Forward Error Correction (FEC) - spend overhead to prevent data loss
- **Game mechanic**: Channels now have failure rates that FEC mitigates

#### 3. Multi-path Routing
- **Cost**: 4 capacity tokens
- **Effect**: Data automatically routes through best available channel
- **Quality of life**: Reduces micromanagement

#### 4. Signal Amplification
- **Cost**: 3 tokens per level
- **Effect**: -5% noise on all channels
- **Max Level**: 8

#### 5. Channel Bonding
- **Cost**: 6 tokens
- **Effect**: Can combine multiple channels for higher throughput
- **Unlock**: New strategic options

- **Effective**: 950 Gbps

### Channel Era Mechanics

#### Noise & Interference
- **New Resource**: Signal Quality (SQ)
- **Generation**: Channels produce data but also generate noise
- **Threshold**: Below 70% SQ, data loss occurs
- **Management**: Spend resources on noise filtering

#### Forward Error Correction (FEC)
- **Trade-off**: Spend 20% bandwidth on redundancy, eliminate 90% of errors
- **Types**:
  - Hamming Code (cheap, 15% overhead)
  - Reed-Solomon (expensive, 30% overhead, 99.9% error correction)
  - Turbo Code (very expensive, 50% overhead, 99.99% correction)

#### Modulation Schemes
- **Upgrades that change channel behavior**:
  - BPSK (basic, 1 bit/symbol)
  - QPSK (2 bits/symbol, +100% throughput, +5% noise)
  - 16-QAM (4 bits/symbol, +300% throughput, +20% noise)
  - 64-QAM (6 bits/symbol, +500% throughput, +50% noise)

## PHASE 4: QUANTUM ERA (Third Rebirth)

### Quantum Mechanics (New Systems)

#### Qubit States
- **Superposition**: Each generator produces qubits in superposed states
- **Collapse**: Measuring converts qubits → classical bits
- **Timing**: When you measure matters - early = less data, late = more decoherence

#### Decoherence
- **Decay**: Qubits lose coherence at rate of 2% per second
- **Loss**: Decoherent qubits = 0 value
- **Strategy**: Balance waiting vs measuring

#### Entanglement
- **Pairing**: Link two generators - they share quantum state
- **Benefit**: Measurement of one gives bonus to other
- **Cost**: 1000 quantum tokens to create entanglement pair

### Quantum Generators

#### 1. Single Qubit Source
- **Cost**: 5 quantum bits (qb)
- **Production**: 1 qubit/s
- **Coherence Time**: 10 seconds

#### 2. Bell Pair Generator
- **Cost**: 100 qb
- **Production**: 15 entangled qubit pairs/s
- **Coherence Time**: 8 seconds
- **Measurement Bonus**: 2x classical bits when measured together

#### 3. GHZ State Preparer
- **Cost**: 2,000 qb
- **Production**: 200 3-qubit GHZ states/s
- **Coherence Time**: 6 seconds
- **Measurement**: 3x bonus if all measured simultaneously

#### 4. Quantum Error Corrector
- **Cost**: 40,000 qb
- **Effect**: Reduces decoherence by 50% on all generators
- **Production**: 0 (support generator)
- **Max**: 3 owned (stacks)

#### 5. Quantum Teleporter
- **Cost**: 800,000 qb
- **Production**: Instant measurement of all qubits
- **Cooldown**: 30 seconds
- **Efficiency**: 95% (5% lost in teleportation)

#### 6. Quantum Computer
- **Cost**: 20,000,000 qb
- **Production**: Runs quantum algorithms
- **Effect**: +500% production to all quantum generators
- **Passive**: Also runs Shor's algorithm (bonus mini-game)

### Quantum Upgrades

#### Coherence Extension

#### Measurement Efficiency
